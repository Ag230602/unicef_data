# AI Assistance Add-on for Submission

## What was added
A new AI Assistance section was integrated into the dashboard (Audio Briefing tab) to provide:

- auto-generated operational briefing text,
- prioritized recommended actions,
- submission-ready disclosure copy.

## Suggested disclosure text
AI-assisted components were used to generate concise dashboard briefings and recommendations from risk metrics. Outputs were reviewed by the project owner before final interpretation. The AI assistance feature is for operational decision support and transparency, not autonomous final decision-making.
